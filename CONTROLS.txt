CONTROLS :-

Movement:
	"W" / UpArrowKey - Move Up
	"S" / DownArrowKey - Move Down
	"A" / LeftArrowKey - Move Left
	"D" / RightArrowKey - Move Right
	"Q" / CloseButton - Closes the game

INSTRUCTIONS :-

Requirements - Python and Pygame

Objective -
    To collect all the coins without getting eaten by the ghosts.
